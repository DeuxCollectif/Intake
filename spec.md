# BA Intake Form - Specification

## What
A form that allows Business Analysts to submit intake requests for new projects or features.

## Why
- Standardize how teams capture project requirements
- Ensure all necessary information is collected upfront
- Create a single source of truth for project initiation

## Scope
- Web-based form with sections for:
  - Project name and description
  - Business requirements
  - Timeline and budget
  - Stakeholder information
  - Success criteria

## Success Criteria
- Form is user-friendly and takes < 10 minutes to complete
- All required fields are validated
- Submissions are stored and retrievable
- 100% of BA intake requests go through this form

## Technical Approach
- Frontend: React
- Backend: Node.js
- Database: PostgreSQL

- Phase 2 (Week 2): Core form development
